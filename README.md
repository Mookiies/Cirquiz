PRIVACY POLICY — CIRCLE QUIZ
Effective Date: March 17, 2026

Thank you for playing Circle Quiz. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle
information in connection with our app. We take your privacy seriously — and in
this case, that means keeping things very simple.


1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Circle Quiz does
not require you to create an account, and we do not gather any data that
identifies you as an individual.


2. THIRD-PARTY QUESTION SOURCES

Circle Quiz retrieves trivia questions from the following third-party APIs:

• Open Trivia Database (OTDB) — opentdb.com
• The Trivia API — the-trivia-api.com

These requests are made solely to fetch questions for gameplay. We do not send
any personally identifiable information to these services. Please refer to their
respective privacy policies for information on how they handle API requests.


3. ANALYTICS AND TRACKING

We do not use any analytics tools, crash reporting services, or third-party
tracking SDKs. Your gameplay activity stays on your device.


4. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Circle Quiz does not collect any information from users of any age. The app is
suitable for all audiences and complies with applicable laws regarding children's
privacy, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).


5. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If our data practices change in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy
and revise the effective date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy
periodically.
